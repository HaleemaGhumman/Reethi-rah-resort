Things To Do At

Reethi Rah

At Your Leisure

From pampering spa sessions to kayaking adventures, romantic beach picnics to family memories made in the sun, One&Only Reethi Rah invites you to do as much or as little as you like.

Explore the Maldives

Set sail across the endless ocean horizon. Dive into the sea for up-close encounters with turtles, sharks and rays, or bask in the Maldives sun on board a canoe, kayak, windsurf and more. Discover why the Maldives is one of the world's most celebrated island destinations for underwater adventures.

An Island of Wonder

Explore all the corners of our 44-hectare island at leisure by foot or by bike. Make your way to Club One, our vibrant hub of activities, and join in on exciting sports, art classes at the Art Studio, or simply sit back and relax in one of our cabanas. Find out how our stunning island can unlock a sense of childlike wonder.

Secrets of Reethi Rah

Let us reveal the secrets of our captivating hideaway, as you explore the mesmerizing underwater world with our Maldivian dive experts, learn about the inspiring work of our sea turtle conservation project, and discover island-grown produce in Botanica restaurant and One&Only Spa.

Sand-Back Family Fun

Dip toes into blue seas, explore the ocean's dazzling underwater world, or build castles in the sand: with so much on offer, family bonding comes naturally at One&Only Reethi Rah. Give them a little independence with our KidsOnly programme, then catch up over a shared family dinner by the beach.

Starry-Eyed Romance

Make the most of your 'together time' with guided dives, chartered cruises and fishing trips during your Maldives honeymoon. Or simply soak up the sun at the adults-only pool, savor a couple's spa treatment, and be sure to round off every day with a celebratory clink of glasses and a memorable meal for two.

Discover Better Wellbeing

Enter a state of deep relaxation with our tailored wellness experiences at One&Only Reethi Rah. With our all-encompassing approach to wellness, you can indulge in bespoke treatments as well as therapeutic journeys inspired by the island. Find your inner balance in our exclusive Watsu pool, followed by a serene, guided meditation.

Immersed in Aqua

With 12 pristine beaches dotted around our Maldives resort, you can hide yourselves away on a different crescent of sand every day. But with a pool for every type, from adults-only to infinity, lounging poolside also holds its own allure.

Explore Islands by Seaplane

See the full beauty of the Maldives laid out below you on a once-in-a-lifetime seaplane excursion. Allow us to arrange this unforgettable experience as you soar across the skies and discover the many islands that make up the beauty of the Maldives.
