About Reethi Rah Resort

Welcome to your magical escape in the Maldives at One&Only Reethi Rah.

Unprecedented privacy. Transcendent luxury. Find privacy with us in a superb all-villa resort in the Maldives. Each villa is a private sanctuary with its own stretch of beach or perched over water with direct lagoon access.

Discover the Location

Surrounded by the wonders of the Indian Ocean, One&Only Reethi Rah makes its home on one of the largest islands in North Male Atoll. A jewel among a string of coral atolls, lagoons and white sands, it is about 700km (430ml) southwest of Sri Lanka.
