Dining at Reethi Rah

Restaurants and Bars

Experience the finest gastronomic journeys within our eight restaurants and five bars. Our exciting curation of dining, bar and lounge options, set in breathtaking ocean venues, will tantalize your senses.

Tapasake

Enter a world of earthly delights overlooking iconic Indian Ocean sunsets and indulge in Tapasake's simple yet artful concept of Japanese-inspired cuisine with a Spanish twist.

Botanica

An atmospheric, outdoor restaurant that heroes outdoor cooking with garden-grown fresh ingredients. Dine beneath the trees and the stars in our farm-to-fork restaurant and be enveloped by the romance of our beautiful hidden gardens. Placing an emphasis on natural ingredients, much of our menu is sourced from our Chef's Garden and the surrounding ocean.

Reethi

Reethi serves bountiful breakfasts in the morning and in the evening, fresh flavors of the Mediterranean coastline in Earth, refined dishes of the French Riviera in Aqua, and pan-Asian cuisine in Fire.

Rabarbaro

Offering authentic and rustic Italian fare with endless ocean views, Rabarbaro is the perfect pittor for a long, lazy lunch and relaxed drinks during the day, and refined Italian dining in the evenings.

Fanditha

The energy of the Middle East comes to life at our exotic, shoreline majlis. Enjoy signature sundowners while watching the waves.

Beach Club

Soak up the energy of the island's southernmost beach while you enjoy healthy snacks or our signature burger from your private cabana.

Private Dining

Choose your gourmet menu and your perfect location in a tailored dining experience curated by our dedicated team just for you and your companions.

Special Dining Events

Mercato Italiano

Delight in a menu reminiscent of the enchanting streets of Italy, complete with homemade pasta, creamy risotto, wood-fired pizza, and succulent porchetta roasted on the spit. Experience an evening of la dolce vita by our pools with chilled Prosecco, Aperol Spritz and live music.

Taste of Soug

Indulge in an enchanting Arabica experience on the shores of Reethi Rah. Savor a variety of authentic Middle Eastern flavors, from live saj and shawarma stations to whole roasted lamb, and unwind to the rhythmic sounds of Maldivian music.

Bubbles and Starlight

Walk barefoot along the beach as you immerse yourself in the vibrant world of live music and Southeast Asian street food. Treat yourself to an array of Pan-Asian delicacies and let our sommeliers pair your meal with chilled Champagne right at your table.
